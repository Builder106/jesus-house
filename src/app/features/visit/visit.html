Come as you are.

No dress code, no expectations — just a church family that's glad you came.

What to expect

We're a warm, multicultural congregation — students, families, and neighbors from many backgrounds worshipping together. You'll be greeted at the door, and nobody will single you out or put you on the spot.

The service runs about 90 minutes, with heartfelt worship and a message from the Bible. Wear whatever you're comfortable in — Sunday best and jeans sit side by side here.

When and where

Sunday Service

9:00 AM

Doors open a little before nine — come early and say hello, or slip in quietly. Either way, you're welcome.

For Wesleyan students

We'll come get you

If you're at Wesleyan and don't have a car, that's no obstacle — our parish picks students up for Sunday service and brings them back to campus afterward. Email or call ahead and we'll have a seat for you.
