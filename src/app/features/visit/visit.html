Your first Sunday

What to expect.

We're a warm, multicultural congregation — students, families, and neighbors from many backgrounds worshipping together. You'll be greeted at the door, and nobody will single you out or put you on the spot.

The service runs about 90 minutes, with heartfelt worship and a message from the Bible. Wear whatever you're comfortable in — Sunday best and jeans sit side by side here.
