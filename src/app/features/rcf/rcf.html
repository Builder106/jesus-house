Who we are

A campus fellowship, run by students.

RCF is the student arm of RCCG Jesus House. It launched at Wesleyan in 2026, led by students from the parish who wanted a place to keep their faith and their friendships growing during the week — not just on Sundays.

Expect worship, honest conversation about the Bible, a few games, and always something to eat. You don't need any church background to belong here — come curious.
